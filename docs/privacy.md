# Privacy Policy — MDX Billing App

MDX Billing App is designed to manage shop billing, products, customers, suppliers, sales, and receipts.

## Data handled

The app may store:
- Shop details
- Product details
- Sales and invoices
- Customer details
- Supplier details
- App settings

## Security

User data must be protected with authentication, database rules, and role-based access control.
